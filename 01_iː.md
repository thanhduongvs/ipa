# `/iː/`
- Make your mouth wide, like a smile. Your tongue touches the side of your teeth.  
![iː](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/thanhduongvs/ipa/main/images/01_nguyen-am-iː.png)

1. (A9b) /iː/ is usually spelled **ee** or **ea**:
- s**ee** `/siː/` *(verb)*
- agr**ee** `/əˈɡriː/` *(verb)*
- **ea**t `/iːt/` *(verb)*
- s**ea**t `/siːt/` *(noun)*
- t**ea**m `/tiːm/` *(noun)*
