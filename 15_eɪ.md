# /eɪ/

1. (A55a) It moves from /e/ to /ɪ/  
![eɪ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/thanhduongvs/ipa/main/images/15_nguyen-am-eɪ.png)

2. (A55b) /eɪ/ is spelled **a**, **ai**, **ay**, **ey**, **ea**, **eigh**:
- **a**ge `/eɪdʒ/` *(noun)*
- c**a**me `/keɪm/`
- pl**a**ne `/pleɪn/` *(noun)*
- t**a**ble `/ˈteɪbl/` *(noun)*
- r**ai**n `/reɪn/` *(noun)*
- w**ai**t `/weɪt/` *(verb)*
- d**ay** `/deɪ/` *(noun)*
- pl**ay** `/pleɪ/` *(verb)*
- s**ay** `/seɪ/` *(verb)*
- gr**ey** `/ɡreɪ/` *(adjective)*
- br**ea**k `/breɪk/` *(verb)*
- gr**ea**t `/ɡreɪt/` *(adjective)*
- **eigh**t `/eɪt/`
- w**eigh**t `/weɪt/` *(noun)*
