# /ɑʊ/

1. (A64a) It moves from /ɑ/ to /ʊ/

2. (A64b) /əʊ/ is spelled **ow**, **ou**:
- h**ow** `/haʊ/` *(adverb)*
- n**ow** `/naʊ/` *(adverb)*
- v**ow**el `/ˈvaʊəl/` *(noun)*
- l**ou**d `/laʊd/` *(adjective)*
- m**ou**th `/maʊθ/` *(noun)*
- s**ou**nd `/saʊnd/` *(noun)*
